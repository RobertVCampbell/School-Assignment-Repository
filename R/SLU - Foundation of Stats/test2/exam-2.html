Problem 3

This problem uses the bechdel data from the fosdata library. Each observation is a movie, and the binary variable tells whether that movie passed or failed the “Bechdel Test”. A movie’s profit (in adjusted 2013 dollars) is the intgross_2013 international gross minus the budget_2013 budget.

Of the 15 least profitable movies in the bechdel dataset, what is the only one to pass the Bechdel test?

fosdata::bechdel %>% mutate(profit=(intgross_2013 - budget_2013)) %>%
  group_by(title) %>% summarize(profit, binary) %>% arrange(profit) %>%
  head(15) %>% filter(binary=="PASS")
